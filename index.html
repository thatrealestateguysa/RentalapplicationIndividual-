Step 2: Complete and sign the forms

  • Question 6 and Question 8 on the KW Explore Questionnaire are not applicable. Leave them blank.
  • The last page of the questionnaire is for office use only. Do not fill in any details there – just sign and initial where indicated.
  • Please sign and initial on each page of all documents you complete.
  • Make sure all personal, employment, and financial information is accurate and legible.

Step 3: Pay the R170 credit check fee

On the rental application form you will see ABSA banking details. Please pay R170 per applicant into this account so we can run a credit check and confirm affordability for the owner.

How the credit check works

  • Pay R170 per applicant using the details alongside.
  • If you are not banking with ABSA, please pay the R170 as an Immediate Payment so that it clears without delay.
  • Email or upload your proof of payment together with your signed forms.
  • We run a credit check on your profile and send the results to the owner of the property.
  • The owner will then decide whether your application is approved. If your credit record is good, there is nothing to stress about – and we will assist you through the process as far as possible.

Step 4: Prepare your supporting documents

Please scan or clearly photograph each document and have them ready to upload or email with your application:

  • Bank confirmation letter.
  • Latest 3 months bank statements.
  • Latest 3 months payslips.
  • Clear copy of your ID (or passport if applicable).
  • Proof of residence (e.g. utility bill, bank statement, etc.).
